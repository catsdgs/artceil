About Us

ArtCiel is a partnership among visual artists, musicians, painters, contractors, and “dreamers” dedicated to creating dramatic visual realities in residential, commercial, and residential spaces. Our shared visual history, your stories, your memories, people’s “pasts, presents, and futures,” are our occupation. Your business is our business. Your idea and imagination—your “space” is special to us. Combining the best of art and technology, ArtCiel is committed to revolutionizing how we create and view our surroundings. Won’t you journey with us? The story begins in summer 2018… More to come…(weekly updates)
