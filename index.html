About Us

ArtCiel is a partnership among visual artists, musicians, painters, contractors, and “dreamers” dedicated to creating dramatic visual realities in residential, commercial, and industrial spaces. Our shared visual history, your stories, your memories, people’s “pasts, presents, and futures,” are our occupation. Your business is our business. Your idea and imagination—your “space” is special to us. Combining the best of art and technology, ArtCiel is committed to revolutionizing how we create and view our surroundings. Won’t you journey with us? The story begins in summer 2018… More to come…(weekly updates)


Our Team

Davin Saderholm

More than an engineer or a seasoned product, process, and business development professional, Davin is equally an artist, creative force, and habitual idea man. Forever seeking a convergence between creativity and industry, Davin has always sought to blend expressive dreams with technology to bring value to humanity. Artciel is just such a dream more than 15 years in the making...

Dominic Barsi

At 17 years old, Dominic Barsi restored a 1928 console radio. That's all it took to start a career of home improvement and technical artistry. From his parents' garage, Dominic began a furniture refurbishing business, and he has blossomed into an artist, painting contractor, and small business owner. He is a performing pianist and drummer who lives and works for connecting emotion with artistic expression, visual beauty, and human experience.

Doug Reynolds

From a young age Doug Reynolds found that what he conceptualized in his mind could become a reality. Where others would find a roadblock, he would build a detour to bring his concepts to fruition. For 25 years he has integrated technologies, designed products and patented ideas and concepts. He has become an expert in many areas of product development and has made innovation and developing new concepts his passion.

Ben Wayman

Ben is a creative director, designer, and artist at heart, hence his interest in Artciel. He has helped companies, large and small, by creating content to build brilliant, impactful, and effective brands. An unconventional thinker, Ben builds strong concepts for digital content, social media, technology, and user experience. With over 20 years in the media world, there's not a problem, large or small, that he hasn't encountered and solved.
